I’m pleased to announce the launching of a new project I'm working on. It's called BotFor.biz
We want to make our chatbots available to everyone.

BFB is designed to enable you introduce artificial intelligence elements into your communication with clients and friends. We have developed an advanced approach to creating a chatbot that can replace a middle-level specialist in such areas as sales, service, medicine, psychotherapy, law and many others.

There are behavior patterns and maps everywhere. For example, in medicine, insurance companies develop a decision map, which brings the doctor's function down to following this card scrupulously. A similar situation also obtains in many other fields.
Chatbot can save you money by communicating with a doctor and a lawyer,
helping companies communicate more effectively with clients.

But we can't cope alone here – we'll need your help in training the chatbot. Each of you has a unique way of communicating and this makes all of us stronger together. Each chatbot trainer will be rewarded with tokens, while each user company will donate tokens for the right to use the system.
